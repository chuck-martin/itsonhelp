View the Products in a Category

Sometimes, such as when you're adding contextual offers to a notification, you'll want to verify which products are in a particular category. When you view a category, you view it in the context of a sandbox. This means that you are viewing the category's settings as it exists as deployed, plus any undeployed changes made to the category in that sandbox.

  1. Go to eCommerce > Catalog & Notifications.
  2. Select the Sandbox view you want to work in. Hover over the down arrow next to the current sandbox name in the header, then click a sandbox name.
  3. Go to Catalog > Categories.
  4. Click the name of a category.
  5. Click the Products tab.

The All Products section lists all products in the category.
