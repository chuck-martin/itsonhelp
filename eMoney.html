eMoney

eMoney is a way of transferring money to subscribers within an account or to other accounts. eMoney contains value that your customers can use to purchase products and services, typically pay-as-you-go services. An analogy to a real-world product might be a gift card.

eMoney cannot be added to compound bundles or exchange categories. If you attempt to promote a change that includes a compound bundle or exchange category that contains an eMoney product, the promotion will fail and you will see an error.

eMoney can go into account wallets or subscriber wallets(*). An account wallet is the same as a customer account balance. A subscriber wallet is where the value of a subscriber's eMoney products is held.

Customers are not allowed to use their account wallet or subscriber wallet to purchase eMoney for their account wallet.

Account Wallet vs. Subscriber Wallet

You can have two types of wallets in an account:

  • An account wallet is used to pay for products and services that you create and offer through ItsOn Smart Services.
  • A subscriber wallet is used to pay for your own pay-as-you-go services.

When an eMoney product is purchased, its value goes to a subscriber wallet if it is being bought for a subscriber in the purchaser's account and to an account wallet if it is being bought for the account or if it is being gifted to a subscriber in another account. In the latter case, a subscriber with account control in the giftee's account can then transfer the eMoney account value to the giftee's subscriber wallet.
