Edit a Subscriber Group

  1. Go to Libraries > Subscriber Groups.

  2. Find the Subscriber Group in the list that you want to edit, and then click the corresponding View button  in the right column. (The buttons in the right column appear when you hover the mouse pointer over the row.)

  3. Click the Unlock button (to unlock and enable editing).
  4. Make any desired changes:

    • Click in the Name field to change the subscriber group name. Names cannot be longer than 32 characters and can contain only letters, number, dashes, and underscores.

    • Click the "x" on any phone number in the Phone numbers field to remove the phone number from the Subscriber Group.
    • Click on a phone number to edit it.
    • Click in an empty area of the Phone Numbers field to enter or paste in additional phone numbers. A subscriber group can contain up to 50 phone numbers.

     See Validation and Fixing Data Errors for more information on entering valid data and fixing invalid data.

  5. Click Save.
The Save button is not active if you have not entered any phone numbers or if you have invalid phone numbers entered and they are not corrected or cleared.
