View the Voice Accounting Policy Used in an eCommerce Voice Service Policy

You can view the details of voice accounting policies on the policy level and on the component level.

  1. Go to Policy > eCommerce Policies.
  2. Find the eCommerce policy that contains the voice service policy with the associated voice accounting policy you want to view, and then click the corresponding View button  in the right column. (The buttons in the right column appear when you hover the mouse pointer over the row.)
  3. Click the Lock button  (to unlock and enable viewing).
  4. Click > View/Edit next to the voice accounting policy.
