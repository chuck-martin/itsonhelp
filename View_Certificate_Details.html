View Certificate Details

The details of an app certificate are the same as what you can see in the Certificates library table, but if any of the information is too long to fit in the display column, you can easily view the entire information.

  1. Go to Policy > Certificates.
  2. Find the certificate in the list that you want to view, and then click the corresponding View button  in the right column. (The buttons in the right column appear when you hover the mouse pointer over the row.)
