Categories for Displaying Plans in the Store

Service Design Center defines a uniquely named category that is a container and an organizer for plans in the store. That category name is SPECIAL (all upper case). When you use the SPECIAL category, the system has the information it needs to display plans in the store.

Sub-categories of the SPECIAL category arrange plans in tabs in a customer's view of the store. The arrangement of plans in those sub-categories determines how they will be arranged in the store. 

For the SPECIAL category to work correctly, the sub-categories must be listed as child categories in the Advanced tab of the SPECIAL category. The order of the sub-categories determines their order in the store. You can always rearrange the sub-categories.

Within the sub-categories, you add plans in the Products tab. The numbers in the Display Order field of the plans in the Products tab determines the plan display order in that sub-category. You can always rearrange the plans.

Also within the sub-categories, you can add dividers. The dividers are sub-categories of the first-level sub-categories. To create dividers, first create them as as categories, and then add them as sub-categories under the Advanced tab as child categories. Then within the divider categories, you add plans in the Products tab.

For example, let's say you have some 1-week data plans, some 2-week data plans, and some 1-month data plans. You already have a Data Plans sub-category of the SPECIAL category, but you don't want all the data plans shoved all together. So you create 3 sub-categories (1 Week Plans, 2 Week Plans, and 1 Month Plans) in the Data Plans category, then put the plans in those sub-sub-categories. It creates a hierarchy that looks something like this:

SPECIAL
  |-- Data Plans
        |-- 1 Week Plans
              |-- 1 Week 1MB Data Plan
              |-- 1 Week 2MB Data Plan
              |-- 1 Week 5MB Data Plan
        |-- 2 Week Plans
              |-- 2 Week 5MB Data Plan
              |-- 2 Week 10MB Data Plan
        |-- 1 Month Plans
              |-- 1 Month 10MB Data Plan
              |-- 1 Month 25MB Data Plan
              |-- 1 Month 50MB Data Plan
              |-- 1 Month 100MB Data Plan
  |-- Voice Plans

etc.
