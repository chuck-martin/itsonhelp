Add Products to a Bundle

Certain product types can and cannot be added to different types of bundles. The following table illustrates this.

  Basic Bundle Compound Bundle

OK to add
  • Plan
  • Fee
  • eMoney
  • Entitlement
  • Value-added service
  • Retail coupon product
  • Plan
  • Entitlement

Do not add
  • Preferred destination change fee
  • External product
  • Basic bundle
  • Compound bundle
  • Fee
  • eMoney
  • Preferred destination change fee
  • External product
  • Value-added service
    Instead of creating a bundle that includes a value-added service product, give a value-added service product as a benefit with a product purchase.
  • Retail coupon product
  • Basic bundle
  • Compound bundle

If you attempt to promote a change that includes a bundle that contains an ineligible product, the promotion will fail and you will see an error message.

  1. Go to Catalog > Products.
  2. Click the name of a bundle.
  3. In the Sku Bundle Items section of the General tab, click Add.
  4. Click Lookup.
  5. Click the name of a plan or fee (but not the name of a compound bundle; compound bundles cannot be added to compound bundles).
  6. Enter a Quantity. (A bundle can contain more than one of a product or fee.)
  7. If you want the product or fee price in the bundle to be different from the price defined in the product or fee settings, enter an Item Sale Price.
  8. Click Save (in the Add Products to Bundle dialog box).
  9. Click Save (on the bundle page).

Service Design Center uses a change management process to track and approve all changes to any product. All of these changes, including additions, edits, and deletions, must go through the promote-approve-deploy process before they become available.

Any changes you make (change a name, change a price, add or delete a product from a bundle, etc.) to any product do not affect any users that already have the product.
