Products: Plans, Bundles, Fees, and Entitlements

Products are the high-level entities where you define everything available to your customers. These entities include:

  • Plans
  • Fees
  • Bundles
  • eMoney
  • Entitlements
  • Value-Added Services
  • Retail Coupons

All of these are listed on the Products page (eCommerce > Catalog & Notifications, then Catalog > Products).

The Products list shows the following information:

Item Description
Name The name of the product. This is a link. Click on the link to see details.
Type

The type of the product. This can be:

  • Product
  • Plan
  • Entitlement
  • Basic Bundle
  • Compound Bundle
  • Preferred Destination Change Fee
  • Fee
  • External Product
  • eMoney
  • Value-Added Service
  • Retail Coupons
Retail Price The price that the product normally sells for.
Sale Price If defined in the product, the price that overrides the retail price.
