Add a Behavior Policy

Behavior policies are stored in their own library. 

  1. Go to Policy > MyFav Behavior Policy.
  2. Click New.
  3. Enter a Name. The policy name should be unique and should be descriptive so it's easy to select from policy lists.
  4. Define the behavior policy settings.
  5. Click Save.

Once a behavior policy has been saved to the Behavior Policy, you can add it to a filter.

Service Design Center allows at most one behavior policy per tenant, and it cannot be deleted once it is created. Once you add a behavior policy, the ability to add more is disabled.
