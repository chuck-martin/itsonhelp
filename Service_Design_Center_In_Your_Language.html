Service Design Center In Your Language

Service Design Center can be presented to you in your native language. The language you see is based on

  • What languages your computer operating system supports
  • What languages your browser supports
  • How your operating system and browser language settings are configured

Language Settings

Service Design Center currently supports the following languages:

  • English
  • Spanish

If you have configured your computer or your browser to display content in either of these languages, then Service Design Center will display its content in your chosen language automatically.

Troubleshooting Language Settings

If Service Design Center does not appear in the language you prefer, you can check your computer and browser language settings.

Both Windows (Control Panel) and Mac (System Preferences) computers have settings specifically for language selection. Make sure that the language set for your computer is the one you want to use. 

Browsers also have language settings, and they override your computer settings. In a browser, you can select multiple languages, and they order them by preference. Where you change these settings differ from browser to browser, but you can usually find them from a Preferences or Settings menu command.
