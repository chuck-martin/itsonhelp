Hidden Products in Bundles

When you offer a bundle for sale, the bundle information in the customer's account shows all the products in the bundle after purchase. However, there may be instances when you want to include hidden products in a bundle. Hidden products do not appear in a customer's account. Hidden products must always have a price equal to 0 (zero).

Normally, this would not be an issue to customers. For example, you could have a bundle of a voice plan, a text plan, and a data plan, and include a hidden bonus data plan. This would appear as a bundle listing three products.

But there is a special case of a bundle with only one product visible. This could be a bundle with one visible product and one hidden product, a bundle with one visible product and three hidden products, or even, for some reason, a bundle with a single product. The ItsOn Smart Services software makes this type of bundle appear as "just" a product in the store. In such a case, make sure you enter the information you want customers to see (including the bundle price) in the bundle name and description fields (see Bundle Settings) because they will not be drawn from the product information. In addition, in this special case, the usage display properties of the one visible product will be what users will see, not the usage display properties set in the bundle.
